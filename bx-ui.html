bx-ui: Blackstone's Enterprise Design System

Building a unified design language for the world's largest alternative asset manager.

Role
Product Designer
Date Range
2022 – 2024
Team
Design Systems Team

Blackstone is a global leader in alternative asset management. With dozens of disparate business units, the need for a cohesive design language was paramount to ensure consistency, accessibility, and efficiency across our digital ecosystem.

Challenge

Blackstone's business units operated in silos, each with their own disparate UI patterns and technologies. This led to inconsistent user experiences, duplicated engineering efforts, and slow product development cycles.

Solution

We created bx-ui, a centralized, scalable, and accessible design system. It emphasizes building a library of reusable components and clear documentation.

Approach

We adopted a collaborative process between the design systems team and product squads to ensure the system met diverse needs while maintaining a unified core.

Tooling & Tech

We leveraged Figma for design, Storybook for engineering documentation, and Design Tokens (via Style Dictionary) as the single source of truth for variables like color, spacing, and typography.

  • Figma: Centralized library for designers.
  • Storybook: Documentation and playground for engineers.
  • Tokens: Platform-agnostic design variables.

Atomic Design

We followed the principles of Atomic Design, breaking down complex UI into smaller, reusable building blocks: Atoms, Molecules, Organisms, and Templates.

Governance

To ensure the long-term success of the design system, we established a clear governance model for contributing new components and making updates. This included a review process and a dedicated Slack channel for support.

Impact

  • Unified 50+ internal and external applications under one design language.
  • Reduced design-to-development handoff time by approximately 30%.
  • Achieved WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility compliance across the enterprise product suite.
