The Alta Logo

Context: Investing in a legacy application again to support the rebranding and launch of Blackstone's new alternative investment platform.

Role: Product Designer

Timeline: January 2024 – February 2024

Challenge

Alta, Blackstone's proprietary investment platform, had a logo that was outdated and lacked the modern, professional feel required for a high-end investment platform. The goal was to create a new logo that reflected the platform's core values of trust, reliability, and innovation.

Goal

The goal of the project was to design a new logo for Alta that would be used across all digital and print materials. The new logo needed to be:

  • Professional and trustworthy, reflecting the high-end nature of the platform and its association with Blackstone.
  • Modern and clean, with a simple and memorable design that would work well at all sizes.

A fresh look for a powerful platform, reflecting the trust and innovation that our users expect and deserve.

Design Principles

To guide the design process, we established a set of design principles that would inform the development of the new logo. These principles included:

  • Clarity: The logo should be simple and easy to read.
  • Trust: The logo should convey a sense of trust and reliability.
  • Innovation: The logo should reflect the platform's focus on innovation and technology.
  • Versatility: The logo should work well across all digital and print materials, from small icons to large banners.
  • Simple and memorable design.
  • Professional and trustworthy color palette.
  • Clean and modern typography.
  • Strong graphic elements.

Process Overview

1. Ideation & Early Sketches — Exploring a wide range of concepts and directions.

Initial sketches focused on exploring different ways to represent the core values of the platform. We explored a wide range of concepts, from abstract symbols to more literal representations of growth and success.

Through this process, we narrowed down the concepts to a few key directions that we felt had the most potential.

2. Refinement — Taking the most promising concepts and refining them based on feedback from stakeholders.

3. Final Design Chosen

Conclusion

  • The new Alta logo is a professional and modern design that reflects the platform's core values of trust, reliability, and innovation.
  • The logo has been well-received by stakeholders and users, and has helped to create a more cohesive and professional brand identity for the platform.
  • The new logo is now being used across all digital and print materials, and has helped to improve the overall user experience of the platform.

The Alta logo project was a successful collaboration between the design team and stakeholders, and has resulted in a logo that accurately reflects the platform's core values and its association with Blackstone.
