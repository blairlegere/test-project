Challenge

Every year, the Rolodex team creates a personalized year-in-review for our users. This year, we wanted to make it even more engaging and insightful than ever before.

Goal

The goal was to create a delightful experience that would encourage users to explore their own data and share it with others.

My Role

As the lead designer, I was responsible for the entire design process, from initial concept to final execution.

  • Conducted user research to understand what users value most.
  • Designed the user interface and user experience for the entire feature.
  • Created all of the data visualizations and illustrations.
  • Collaborated with the engineering team to ensure the feature was implemented correctly.

How do you create delightful insights from user statistics, and how does that feature evolve with time?

Approach

I started by researching other year-in-review features, such as Spotify Wrapped and Duolingo Year in Review. I also conducted user interviews to understand what users were most interested in seeing in their own data.

  • Who did you communicate with most this year?
  • What was your most active month for networking?
  • How does your network compare to last year?
